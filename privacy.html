Privacy / Information

This website embeds videos from YouTube using an iframe (`youtube-nocookie`). Video content is provided by YouTube/Google.

Third-party processing

When you view an embedded video, YouTube/Google may collect and process information (such as device and browser data and usage data) according to their own policies. This website does not control how YouTube processes that information.

“nocookie” note

Using `youtube-nocookie` helps reduce certain tracking behaviors that may occur before you interact with the embedded content. However, third-party processing may still happen depending on your browser settings and how the embedded video is accessed.

Our data

This site does not use analytics, advertising cookies, or other tracking technologies. It is not intended to collect personal data through scripts on this website.
